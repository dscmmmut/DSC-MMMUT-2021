About MMMUT

 

Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology, popularly known as MMMUT , is a Autonomous University in Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, India. MMMUT has been established in year 2013 by the Government of Uttar Pradesh in the form of a non-affiliating, teaching and research University after reconstituting the Madan Mohan Malaviya Engineering College, Gorakhpur which was established in 1962.
